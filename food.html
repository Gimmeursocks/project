Restaurants in EGYPT

Egypt is the city in which you find everything, distinguished by all its tourist components that made it few for all tourists, and among those components are restaurants, where it has a wonderful group of restaurants that offer various types of food to enjoy during tourism in Egypt. Eating authentic Egyptian food is an irreplaceable opportunity in one of Egypt's beautiful restaurants, which vary to satisfy all tastes of visitors, and in addition to Egyptian dishes, there are restaurants in Egypt that serve wonderful Asian food and the most famous international kitchens

Abu El Sid Restaurant

Abou El Sid Restaurant is one of the most famous Egyptian restaurants in Cairo. It serves authentic Egyptian cuisine that dates back to the Fatimid state, and the menu includes a variety of appetizers. The atmosphere of the restaurant is charming with its woodwork, overhead lighting, and classical Egyptian music. There are large tables for groups. It has many branches. Between the traditional atmosphere and the folkloric form of design and decoration, Abu El Sid Restaurant, one of the most beautiful restaurants in Cairo, will impress you, and is located in a number of areas of the Egyptian capital, Cairo. Which gives the place a special charm.

Kaddoura fish restaurant

Kaddoura Restaurant is a famous name in the world of Egyptian restaurants that offers the best fish meals. When you enter the restaurant, you head to a place that resembles a fish market, and choose what you like from the fish, then choose the method of cooking the fish you prefer, grilled, fried or casserole, without a menu as if you were at home. If you are a fan of seafood and fresh fish, dear traveler, do not hesitate to visit Qadwa Fish Restaurant during your visit to the Egyptian capital, Cairo. Prepare it in the best way.

Koshary Abu Tarek

For the most beautiful experience of discovering Egyptian koshary, head to Abu Tarek Restaurant in the city center. Since 1950, Abu Tarek has been photographed on CNN and is now famous all over the world for preparing this famous dish, which consists of a mixture of rice and pasta. And lentils, with a spicy tomato sauce garnished with chickpeas and crunchy fried onions, with garlic vinegar or chili sauce.

The revolving restaurant at the Grand Hyatt

It is one of the famous Cairo restaurants that embraces the sky with its high altitude, to complete its 360-degree rotations every 75 minutes with the clock, during which its lights are reflected on the waters of the Nile to paint the breathtaking painting with the three pyramids with an incomparable charm. A unique experience full of fun and adventure. The revolving restaurant is located on the 41st floor of the Grand Hyatt Hotel (Grand Nile Tower Cairo), which is one of the most luxurious hotels in Cairo, which represented an important part in the tourism sector in Egypt. The revolving restaurant is one of the first outstanding restaurants in the Middle East, which won the best performance for 2012 And it was crowned according to TripAdvisor, which granted it this certificate after a survey among its goers, thus gaining its worthy place among Cairo's five-star restaurants.

Restaurants in GREECE

The city of Athens, the Greek capital, is one of the most important tourist cities in Greece. It has many tourist places and historical and archaeological sites, in addition to many picturesque tourist areas. In addition to all this, it also enjoys a group of high-end hotels. The most important characteristic of Athens hotels is the good service and comfort for guests. In addition to the distinctive Athens hotels, you will also find a group of Athens restaurants that come at the forefront of the following list that we recommend to you among the best restaurants in Athens

Byzantine Restaurant

This restaurant is located in the Hilton Athens, located in the heart of the city. It serves a variety of delicious dishes, especially for breakfast and lunch, including some traditional Greek dishes known for their delicious taste. Indeed, it is one of the most luxurious and famous restaurants in Athens.

spondi restaurant

This restaurant has two Michelin stars, and it is one of the most prominent restaurants that serve the most delicious dishes, taking you back to the ancient era in terms of the traditional and famous dishes of Greece. In addition to the antique decorations that make you feel that time has gone back in time. It is worth mentioning that this restaurant has won many international awards thanks to the skilled chef “Arnaud Bignon”. The restaurant is located in the Olympic arena, which kicks off the Olympic Games. Therefore, the restaurant is very popular and offers everything that is fresh and delicious, according to the fresh foods available in the markets every day.

Nargile Kefalari Restaurant

It is one of the famous Lebanese restaurants in Athens, and is known for its oriental decorations and authentic Arabic ambiance. The view of Athens on the Mediterranean Sea always makes it the link between the Arab and European peoples. It really represents the link in the cultural interdependence between countries. As for the dishes offered by the restaurant, there is nothing wrong with the diversity of dishes, especially the Lebanese and oriental dishes, in addition to a variety of Western dishes.

Mecca Restaurant

This restaurant is originally Indian and Pakistani and serves a great selection of delicious dishes. And add to all this that all the dishes it offers are “halal”, and that is why it attracts a large number of Arab and Muslim tourists. When you enter the restaurant, you feel at ease, as its decorations are very simple, which makes you feel comfortable, which increases when tasting the most delicious meals. If you are a fan of eating dishes from the Indian cuisine, this restaurant offers a very distinctive selection and will be the perfect choice for you. It is one of the distinctive Athens restaurants.

Restaurants in ITALY

It is a destination for food lovers, where the fence goes to enjoy Italian food, and the distinctive spirit that dominates its cafes and restaurants, so here are the best and most famous restaurants in Italy to try on your next visit.

Enoteca Pinchiorri Restaurant

The restaurant is considered one of the most famous Italian restaurants, and the most important thing that distinguishes it is that the chef responsible for cooking in the restaurant is the chef “Annie Feld”, the first woman in Italy to excel in cooking. A 3-star restaurant, the restaurant is located in the charming city of Florence, and the restaurant contains the most delicious Italian innovative dishes of risotto, saffron and dried fruits

Disco Restaurant

Desco restaurant is one of the best Italian restaurants and contains the best and cheapest menu in Italy. Il Desco restaurant is unique in its delicious sauces such as the wonderful caramel sauce and the delicious lettuce sauce. The restaurant is located in the Italian city of Verona.

El Villagio Restaurant

Il Villaggio restaurant is located inside the Italian “Four Seasons” hotel, and the chef “Vito Monica” makes the best culinary menu in Italy, and among the delicious dishes that the chef offers is the “Mushroom Risotto” “Chateaubrian Foie Gras” and many other wonderful Italian dishes.

Restaurant Da Fortunato

The restaurant is located in the center of Rome where millions of tourists and celebrities come to it. The restaurant serves traditional Italian cuisine such as fresh fish, spaghetti and pastries with exquisite seashells with garlic and hot red pepper. In addition to these delicious dishes, you will find desserts such as ice cream, pear ice cream, caramelized walnuts, and many other delicious dishes.

Restaurants in INDIA

There are good restaurants, and there are other wonderful ones that make dining an enjoyable and distinctive experience. The wonderful restaurants transform the catering service into something closer to the fine arts, and they are always keen to raise the level of service provided to be able to survive and continue successfully in light of the great competition between restaurants, especially in countries Like India where people are very appreciative of good food. High-end restaurants in India have large numbers, but some of them have been able to thrive and gain the loyalty of many customers thanks to their distinction from other high-end restaurants

Indian Accent

Located in a small boutique hotel, The Manor, Indian Accent is an innovative and unusual restaurant in New Delhi where Chef Manish Mehrotra prepares local foods with contemporary international cuisine in new and unexpected ways, using roasted coconut and pickled tomatoes, with the addition of Roasted peanuts, peanut butter and other local ingredients.

Muti Mahal

Moti Mahal is located in the Daryaganj area, and is one of the oldest and wonderful historical restaurants in Delhi. It dates back to 1947, shortly after Indian independence. It stands out among other Indian restaurants for its amazing recipes, chicken dishes and wonderful kebabs, and it is even a great place to savor These are North Indian dishes, and many of the city's visitors come to try the most delicious Indian delicacies.

Bukhara

This amazing restaurant is ranked as one of the top 50 Asian restaurants, as well as one of the best restaurants in New Delhi and India in general, thanks to its distinguished meals in a perfect and unforgettable atmosphere, from the world-renowned kebabs to the amazing kofta that it has been famous for since its inception in 1977.

Punjabi by Nature Restaurant

Punjabi by Nature Restaurant is another restaurant that is very popular among tourists and locals in New Delhi, even now it is expanding to multiple locations across the city, and as its name suggests, it specializes in preparing delicious meals with the finest fresh ingredients that add the original taste of the dishes Including lamb and black lentils with butter and cream, as well as chickpeas, potatoes and onions.

Restaurant El Brillante

One of the most famous dishes in Madrid is the Bocadillo de Calamares: a baguette filled with fried squid rings, there is nowhere better known for this dish than El Brillante. Restaurants for diners have faded from Spain in the past 15 years, most shops closed and replaced with more trendy venues, but El Brillante is still a testament to its quality. Located in front of the Atocha train station, near the Reina Sofa Museum, almost every visitor to the Spanish capital passes by this restaurant. It might seem a little scary at first, with the bar staff shouting orders across the room to the kitchen, but that's part of the charm of the place.

Restaurants in TURKEY

Istanbul includes wonderful and diverse restaurants that cater to all tastes, and offer the most delicious dishes from the famous Turkish cuisine, in addition to the most delicious international and Arab dishes. There are restaurants overlooking the sea and distinguished by their wonderful location, Taksim restaurants, and popular cheap restaurants, as well as luxury restaurants in European and Asian Istanbul. The ancient mix of cultures has had a huge impact on the food scene in Istanbul, where you can enjoy different cuisines from authentic Ottoman food, with its delicious mezze, to Turkish cuisine with its famous grills. If you are a lover of baklava, also do not miss to try the traditional Turkish dessert, this is the right place.

Suda Kebab Restaurant

Suda Kebap Restaurant is one of the best restaurants in Istanbul. It is famous for its diverse local dishes, which include a wide variety of Turkish cuisine, especially the famous Antabian cuisine, in addition to dessert dishes and others. Souda Kebab Restaurant serves different local cuisines in an elegant and friendly atmosphere that can be sensed from the interior design of the restaurant, where it welcomes its visitors with its wide tables and wooden floors. Here you will find luxurious items ranging from all kinds of kebabs to harissa and special baklava, and you can enjoy delicious foods and desserts. Kitchen in Gaziantep.

Nusrat Istanbul Restaurant

Nusrat Steak House Istanbul, famous in Turkish as Nusr-Et Etiler, is one of the most important and luxurious Istanbul restaurants specializing in wonderful meat and steaks. It is reported that its owner, Nusret Gökçe, of Turkish origin, was living in America and mastered the art of cooking and grilling meat and was creative in it. He opened his famous restaurant, Nusret Restaurant in Istanbul in 2010 AD, so that his restaurant was classified as the best in serving grilled meat, and this is what made him open other branches in Istanbul, Ankara and Dubai as well

Restaurants in FRANCE

The city of Paris, the French capital and the most beautiful cities of France, the city of lights and the capital of fashion, not to mention being the most important destination for lovers of shopping and fashion.

Nora Paris Restaurant

Noura Restaurant in Paris is a Lebanese restaurant that serves various types of oriental Arabic foods such as tabbouleh, arais, pastries and kibbeh, in addition to sweets and ice cream. It is considered the best Arabic restaurant in Paris and has many branches, one of which is on the famous Champs Elysees Street. The restaurant has a beautiful atmosphere and elegant decor. The staff at Noura's restaurant are mostly Arabs.

Al-Ajami Restaurant, Paris

Al-Ajami Restaurant in Paris is distinguished by its oriental dishes, and it is a Lebanese restaurant that features a steak dish. The prices in this restaurant are a bit high, but the food it serves is considered the most delicious, and it is one of the most famous Arab restaurants in Paris. The restaurant is located on a street off George V Avenue, King Francois I Street.

Pino Pizza Restaurant

Pino Pizza Restaurant is one of the most famous restaurants in Paris that specialize in Italian dishes. The restaurant is very suitable for lovers of pizza and pasta on the Italian way. It is located near the George V station

Pasco Restaurant

One of the best restaurants in Paris that serves Mediterranean dishes in addition to the delicious meals of the southern region of France. It provides a rich and varied menu that includes simple yet delicious meals, reasonable prices, and offers recipes in new ways and different flavors.
