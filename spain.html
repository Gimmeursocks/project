Tourism in Madrid

Madrid, the Spanish capital, is considered one of the most beautiful cities in Spain. Tourism is famous for its tourist attractions, especially the historical ones. The city includes many palaces and museums. Many of the pioneers of tourism in Spain take the capital, Madrid, as the first stop on their trip, as it is famous for its distinctive restaurants and cafes, and includes many buildings with high-end architectural design.

Museo del Prado

The Museo del Prado is the main national museum in Spain and one of the oldest places of tourism in Madrid. The history of the museum dates back to the eighteenth century and includes inside it a large amount of works and paintings by the most important international artists, which belong to European art between the twelfth and nineteenth centuries.

Tourism in Granada

Granada is one of the most famous historical Andalusian cities. It is one of the mountainous cities of Spain. It rises about 700 meters above sea level. It is located on the slopes of the Sierra Nevada Mountains. The city of Granada is one of the most important tourist areas in Spain because of its prominent Islamic landmarks, such as buildings and interesting cobblestone streets.

Alhambra Palace

The Alhambra Palace in Granada is considered one of the most important tourist places in Granada. It is an ancient palace that dates back to the year 889. It was at that time an impregnable fortress until it was converted into a royal palace by the Sultan of Granada Yusuf I.

Albayazin district

One of the most beautiful tourist places in Granada, which is an old Andalusian neighborhood that still retains its Andalusian identity. The Albayazin neighborhood is one of the most famous tourist destinations in Granada, Spain, where many tourists go to see its historical landmarks that are embodied in its narrow streets and beautiful buildings. The neighborhood is classified by UNESCO as a World Heritage Site

Granada Cathedral

The cathedral is one of the most famous landmarks of Granada, Spain. It is characterized by its architectural design that combines the Gothic and Renaissance styles. The cathedral was built over the Granada Mosque, which was built by the Red Banu, who were the princes of Granada at the time after seizing the city in 1492, and it is now one of the most important tourist places in Granada.

Science Park

A great place for a picnic with the family that combines knowledge and entertainment, the Science Park is a science museum that attracts many visitors of all ages. The museum is located on an area of ​​70,000 square meters and is one of the most important tourist attractions in Granada and the first museum of its kind in Spain. The museum includes many sections, including the astronomical observatory, the physics and mechanics department, an interactive section dedicated to experiments that are suitable for children, and other sections.

Archaeological Museum of Granada

This museum is considered one of the most important tourist attractions in Granada, as it includes many rare artifacts. The Archaeological Museum of Granada is located in a historic palace dating back to the 16th century AD. It consists of two floors and displays many civilizations that ruled the region, including the Roman, Phoenician, Carthaginian, Arab and other civilizations.

Corporal's paradise

The Generalife, located inside the Red Palace, is one of the most beautiful places of tourism in Granada, Spain, as it attracts many visitors who seek calm and relaxation amid the beautiful scenery it contains. This group of gardens is located in a high place so that it can be seen from all over the city of Granada

Sierra Nevada National Park

One of the most famous national parks in the city of Granada, Spain, and it is part of a nature reserve. The park includes wonderful natural scenes represented by rivers, lakes and snow-covered mountains. It also contains many animals that live in the mountainous environment. The park is one of the most beautiful places of tourism in Granada, Spain, and attracts many tourists, especially lovers of snowboarding and mountaineering.

Tourism in Seville

Seville, the capital of Andalusia, is located in the south of Spain and constitutes a distinctive mixture in which Spanish culture is mixed with Moroccan culture in North Africa. It is undoubtedly one of the most beautiful tourist cities in Spain, for its cultural and historical richness, and the large number of ancient tourist attractions in it

Al Mawarq Palace

A historical palace built by the Almoravid Arabs in the city of Seville, Spain, in the late first millennium by the Arab rulers of Seville. The Spaniards continued to develop it after the fall of the Islamic state in Andalusia, to become a symbol of the city of Seville The palace is distinguished by the beauty of its architecture, design and wonderful gardens. It is undoubtedly one of the most important tourist attractions in Seville

Santa Cruz area

One of the most beautiful tourist places in Seville, Spain, this historical area is located east of the city on the borders of the Grand Valley River.

colorful solar metropol

One of the most beautiful tourist attractions in Seville, Spain. It was completed in 2004. It is a wonderful masterpiece located in a square in the old town area of Seville. The building includes 6 structures that form a large canopy made of imported Finnish birch wood.

Maria Luisa Garden

Maria Luisa Park extends along the Guadalquivir River near the center of Seville, Spain, and is considered one of the most important tourist attractions in the city.

Giralda Tower

La Giralda Tower in Seville is one of the most important tourist places in Spain. It is a minaret of the Great Mosque during the Almohad era during the Islamic state of Andalusia.

Seville cathedral

This cathedral is considered the most famous tourist attraction in Seville, Spain. It was built from 1401-1519 on the site of the Jameh Mosque, where it was converted into a cathedral after the end of the Muslim rule of Andalusia. The cathedral was built in the Gothic style and is now the third largest cathedral in Europe in terms of size

Casa de Pilatos Palace

La Casa de Pilatos is a true embodiment of the form taken by medieval buildings, the palace is also known among the people of Seville as the Palace of the Andalusians. The palace is a tourist attraction in Seville and is the home of the Rivera family, who ruled Seville and its territory in the sixteenth century.

Tourism in the Canary Islands

The Canary Islands is one of the most beautiful tourist destinations in Spain, as it embraces unusual landscapes that enchant the eye and enjoy a mild climate all year round. The Canary Islands include a number of UNESCO World Heritage sites, the third tallest volcano in the world, and many recreational sites

According to statistics, the number of people who come for tourism in the Canary Islands has reached nearly 12 million visitors annually, especially Gran Canaria and Tenerife.

Activities you can do

• You can visit El Hierro Island, which is one of the most beautiful Canary Islands and is considered a world ocean reserve on the UNESCO World Heritage List, and includes stunning beaches such as El Golfo Beach.

• You can also visit one of the best islands, which is the island of Fuerteventura, the second largest and oldest of the Canary Islands, which is also a nature reserve, and includes buildings dating back to the Middle Ages.

• Gran Canaria is the most visited Canary Islands, and includes many entertainments that you can enjoy such as Palmetos Bird Park, Maspalomas Lighthouse

• The island of La Gomera includes the Garagonay National Park, which was included in the UNESCO list of World Heritage Sites in 1986 AD, and occupies 40 square kilometers of its area, and is considered one of the most beautiful tourist areas in the Canary Islands

• You must visit the unique Malvasia vineyards on the island of Lanzarote, and Papagayo Beach, which is characterized by its calm waves and clear waters

• You can also visit the William Herschel Observatory, which is located above Mount de los Mochachos, the highest peaks of the islands, especially the island of La Palma.

• Do not forget to visit the island of Tenerife, which includes several tourist resorts such as Playa de las Americas, Siam Water Park, and the Opera House.
